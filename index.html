📖 Sample Essay

Some people argue that parents should be responsible for teaching children how to become responsible members of society, while others believe that schools are better suited for this role. Although educational institutions play an important part in children’s development, I believe that parents have a more significant influence on shaping their character and social values.

On the one hand, schools provide a structured environment in which children can acquire essential knowledge and social skills. Through standardized curricula and interaction with peers, students are exposed to diverse perspectives and learn how to cooperate with others.

On the other hand, parents play a crucial role in guiding children’s moral development. From an early age, children are strongly influenced by their family environment, where they learn fundamental values such as empathy, responsibility, and respect.

In conclusion, although both schools and parents play important roles, parental influence is more decisive as it forms the foundation of a child’s development.
